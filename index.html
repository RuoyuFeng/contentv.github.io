Abstract

We present ContentV, an advanced video generation system capable of creating high-quality, coherent and diverse videos.

ContentV accelerates DiT-based video generation through three key innovations: (1) a minimalist model design that enables effective reuse of pre-trained image generation models for video synthesis; (2) a comprehensive exploration of a multi-stage, efficient training strategy based on Flow Matching; and (3) a low-cost Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback (RLHF) approach that further enhances generation quality without the need for additional human annotations.

With only four weeks of training on 256 64GB AI accelerators, our 8B model achieves state-of-the-art performance in video generation. Specifically, it scored 85.14 on VBench and is only behind Wan2.1-14b among open-source models. All code and models are publicly available.
